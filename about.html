About Us

The Wedding Planners are Mumbai-based wedding planners that provide a one-stop solution for your wedding and related events. As a wedding planner in India, we bring unique touches to weddings, focusing on executing your special day down to the last detail. This means that we take care of presenting you with only the most suitable venues and vendors according to your budget and taste. We at Marry Me work closely with you — the couple — and your families to plan a wedding that is uniquely yours and that is a reflection of your tastes. This means that whether you opt for a small and intimate wedding, a large-scale production, or even an exotic destination wedding, we will make your wedding day the most special and unique — one that will be remembered forever. We take pleasure in the fact that we have been able to create memorable moments by blending Indian tastes and tradition with contemporary touches.
